🚨 ACCESS BLOCKED ERROR? DO THIS NOW:

1. Go to: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/credentials/consent

2. Scroll to "Test users" section

3. Click "+ ADD USERS"

4. Enter your Google email

5. Click "SAVE"

6. Try signing in again - SHOULD WORK! ✅
